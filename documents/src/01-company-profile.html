We replace that with intelligent systems that work 24 hours a day, in English, Kiswahili and Arabic.

Section 02What We Build
Our Systems

Five systems. One outcome: more placements.

01 · The 5-Minute Candidate Response System

Every enquiry — WhatsApp, Facebook, website — answered, qualified and routed within minutes, 24/7, in three languages. Responding within 5 minutes makes an agency up to 10× more likely to win the candidate.

02 · The Document Compliance Engine

Passports, IDs, Good Conduct certificates, medicals, Yellow Fever cards — digitised, checklist-enforced per destination country, and expiry-monitored with alerts before a document expires, never after.

03 · The Candidate Journey Autopilot

From registration to departure, every candidate moved along automatically — medical reminders, interview confirmations, visa updates — with a warm, human tone. Drop-offs flagged to your team instantly.

04 · The Dormant Candidate Goldmine

Your old database is unclaimed revenue. We clean, segment and re-engage past candidates and cold enquiries with personalised outreach — recovering placements at zero ad spend.

05 · The Director's Daily Briefing

Every morning at 7:00 AM the director receives the full state of the agency on Telegram — new candidates, pending documents, appointments, overdue tasks. Ask anything, answered in seconds.

⚜ The Agency Brain™ — Flagship

All five systems unified into one intelligent operations engine. The director commands it on Telegram; candidates are served on WhatsApp; the whole agency runs awake 24 hours a day. Self-learning — it grows with your business.
